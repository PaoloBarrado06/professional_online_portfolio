Mechanics of the game

In-Between is a famous card game in the Philippines where a player will be given two (2) cards. If he takes the deal, the player will be given a third card. The number or value of the third card needs to be in between the two cards that were shown at the start. If the card is in-between, then you won the about you have bet. If the first two cards are the same, you will guess if the third card will be higher or lower.
